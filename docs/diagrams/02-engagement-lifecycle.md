# CSA Helper — Engagement Lifecycle

The end-to-end journey of a customer engagement, from intake to expansion.

```mermaid
flowchart LR
    A[Intake & Outcomes] --> B[Scoping & RAID]
    B --> C[VBD / Delivery Plan]
    C --> D[Architecture Design]
    D --> E[Security Gate]
    E --> F[Delivery Execution]
    F --> G[Milestone Coaching]
    G --> H[Closeout & Hygiene]
    H --> I[Impact Reporting]
    I --> J[Post-Delivery Expansion]
    J -.feedback.-> A
```
